Quick rules

Use this like an app at the gym. If anything feels wrong, reduce range/resistance or stop.

Acceptable

  • 0–3/10 discomfort during exercise is OK if it settles within 24 hours.
  • Mild muscular burn/fatigue is expected on band work.

Stop & adjust

  • Sharp pain, catching, giving-way, or pain that escalates rep-to-rep.
  • Numbness/tingling or new weakness.
  • Next-day flare: reduce band/ROM next time.

Day 1 • Hip activation + shoulder control

Glute/hip control (bands) + rotator cuff/scapula stability. Keep reps slow and pain-free.

High level

  • Hip: banded activation to calm the hip and build control.
  • Shoulder: scapular + cuff control with light band (no shrugging).

Resisted Bridge (band above knees)

✓
Dose: 10 reps × 1 set, 5-sec hold at top (confirm sets with physio). Band: Medium band Focus: smooth + controlled

Band guidance

Open in YouTube ↗

Setup

  • Band just above knees; feet hip-width; ribs down; pelvis neutral.

Start

  • Create light band tension by gently pressing knees out.
  • Brace core (ribs down, no back arch).

Move

  • Drive through heels; lift hips until shoulders–hips–knees line up.
  • Knees track over mid-foot (don’t cave in).

Peak

  • Squeeze glutes hard for 5 seconds; keep lower back relaxed.

Feel

  • Glutes + hamstrings (not front of hip or low back).

Avoid

  • Over-arching low back at the top.
  • Feet too far/too close causing cramps.
  • Knees collapsing despite band.

Progression: Single-leg supported bridge

Only progress if

  • Basic bridge feels easy and stable (no back arch).
  • Pain stays ≤3/10 and settles within 24h.
  • Physio has cleared you to progress.
Dose: 8 reps × 1 set each side, 3-sec hold. Band: {"none":"No band","light":"Light band","medium":"Medium band","heavy":"Heavy band"}[pb]

Setup

  • Same as bridge; extend one leg (or keep toe lightly on floor for support).

Start

  • Level hips—imagine headlights on your hips pointing straight up.

Move

  • Drive through heel of working leg; lift slowly; lower slowly.

Peak

Resisted Hip Extension + External Rotation (prone)

✓
Dose: 10 reps × 1 set. Band: Light band Focus: smooth + controlled

Band guidance

Open in YouTube ↗

Setup

  • Lie on stomach; legs straight; band as prescribed.
  • Keep pelvis heavy on floor; gentle abdominal brace.

Start

  • Rotate leg outward slightly from hip (not pelvis).

Move

  • Lift leg a few inches using glute; 2 sec up / 2 sec down.

Peak

  • Stop before pelvis tips or low back arches (small lift is enough).

Feel

  • Glute max/hamstring + deep hip rotators.

Avoid

  • Lifting too high and arching back.
  • Rotating whole pelvis instead of hip.

Progression: Quadruped hip extension (donkey kick) with band

Only progress if

  • You can keep pelvis still in prone version.
  • No pinching at front of hip.
Dose: 10 reps × 1 set each side. Band: {"none":"No band","light":"Light band","medium":"Medium band","heavy":"Heavy band"}[pb]

Setup

  • Brace gently; keep spine neutral.

Start

Move

  • Press heel up/back without arching low back.

Peak

  • Stop when pelvis wants to tilt.

Feel

  • Glute on working side.

Avoid

  • Rib flare/low back arch.
  • Hip opening out.

Resisted Straight Leg Raise (SLR)

✓
Dose: 10 reps × 1 set. Band: Light band Focus: smooth + controlled

Band guidance

Open in YouTube ↗

Setup

  • Lie on back; one knee bent, other leg straight; toes pulled up.

Start

  • Tighten thigh to keep knee fully straight (quad on).

Move

  • Lift straight leg to comfortable range (often 30–45°).
  • Lower slowly (2–3 seconds).

Peak

  • Pause briefly; pelvis stays level (no hiking).

Feel

  • Front thigh + mild core work.

Avoid

  • Knee bending.
  • Ribs flaring / low back arching.

Progression: SLR with ankle weight

Only progress if

  • You can do 10 reps with perfect knee lock and no hip pinch.

Scapular Retraction (band around wrists)

✓
Dose: 10 reps × 1 set, 5-sec hold. Band: Light band Focus: smooth + controlled

Band guidance

Open in YouTube ↗

Setup

  • Stand tall; band around wrists; elbows soft; neck long.

Start

  • Let shoulders drop away from ears.

Move

  • Pull shoulder blades back AND down; hold squeeze.
  • Keep ribs stacked (no back arch).

Peak

  • Strong squeeze between shoulder blades for 5 seconds.

Feel

  • Mid/lower traps + rhomboids.

Avoid

  • Shrugging.
  • Over-arching low back to ‘cheat’.

Progression: Band row (elbows close)

Only progress if

  • No shoulder pain; you can retract without shrugging.
Dose: 12 reps × 1 set. Band: {"none":"No band","light":"Light band","medium":"Medium band","heavy":"Heavy band"}[pb]

Setup

  • Band anchored chest-height; stand tall.

Start

  • Shoulders down/back; wrists neutral.

Move

  • Row elbows back; pause; return slowly.

Peak

  • Squeeze shoulder blade back/down.

Feel

  • Mid-back; not neck.

Avoid

  • Shrugging or rib flare.

Day 3 • Mobility / yoga / stretches

Restore motion and calm symptoms. Slow breathing, never force range. Mild stretch only.

High level

  • Mobility day: longer exhales, gentle range.
  • If tight: do less range, longer breathing.

Hip Mobility Combo (Adductors → Hip Flexors → Pigeon)

✓
Dose: 3 reps × 1 set, 30-sec hold each. Band: No band Focus: smooth + controlled

Band guidance

Open in YouTube ↗

Setup

  • Move slowly between positions; breathe long exhales.

Start

  • Adductor: side lunge with straight leg; toes forward.

Move

  • Hip flexor: drop back knee; squeeze back-leg glute.
  • Then pigeon: keep hips as square as possible.

Peak

  • Each hold: mild–moderate stretch only; relax jaw/shoulders.

Feel

  • Inner thigh → front hip → glute stretch.

Avoid

  • Dumping into low back in hip flexor stretch.
  • Forcing knee angle in pigeon.

Progression: Longer holds (45–60s) + 3 gentle contract-relax pulses

Only progress if

  • 30s holds feel comfortable and symptoms settle well.
Dose: 2 reps × 45–60s each position. Band: {"none":"No band","light":"Light band","medium":"Medium band","heavy":"Heavy band"}[pb]

Setup

  • Settle into position.

Start

  • Exhale to relax deeper.

Move

  • Contract lightly 3 seconds, relax 6 seconds (3 cycles).

Peak

  • Never force; stay pain-free.

Feel

  • Gradual easing.

Avoid

  • Aggressive pushing.

Day 4 • Cardio (pain-free)

Low impact conditioning. Stop if pain spikes or symptoms worsen during/after.

High level

  • Low-impact cardio only.
  • Stop if pain increases during session.
